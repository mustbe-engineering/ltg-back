It all starts with an idea. In this video we talk with Vanessa, founder of Ladies The Gathering, and with participants from this community created for girls seeking a space to learn, play, and connect through Magic: The Gathering. From Tijuana, we discuss how LTG was born, the sense of belonging, the importance of creating a safe and inclusive space, the community that has formed among new and experienced players, and where this project is headed. We made this collaboration with love.
