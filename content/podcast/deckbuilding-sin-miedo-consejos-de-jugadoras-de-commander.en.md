Did your first deck not work out as you expected? Welcome to the club.

In this episode, Michelle and Daniela share how their first steps in Commander went: the mistakes, the precons that changed their lives, and how they improved their decks card by card.

We talk about why a precon is the best place to start, how to improve without breaking the bank, and why deckbuilding itself is another way to enjoy Magic.

If you feel like building a deck is intimidating, this episode is for you.
