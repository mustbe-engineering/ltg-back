In this episode of Magic is for Everyone, we had the opportunity to invite Vanessa from "*Ladies the Gathering*", an MTG community exclusive to Women+ where they can share a safe space to learn and play. 

We discuss:

✨ How the idea for a group in Tijuana was born

✨ Experience from their first "Commander Tea Party" event

✨ Major challenges in creating Ladies the Gathering

✨ Commentary and experiences from women who play Magic in the Tijuana community

Whether you're new to Magic, an experienced player, or looking to start playing competitively, this episode is full of practical examples and anecdotes that will help you improve your competitive game and enjoy your matches even more.
