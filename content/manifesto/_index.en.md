title: The Community Manifesto
quote: >-
  At Ladies The Gathering, we take immense pride in being a community like no
  other. In such a chaotic world, the true beauty of our group lies in welcoming
  every woman who has ever felt vulnerable or unwelcome in TCG spaces. Because
  of this, we have zero tolerance for disrespect—whether it happens within our
  ranks or outside of them. For us, sisterhood is the foundation. There is no
  better feeling than knowing you are truly among friends.


  If you would like to be part of Ladies The Gathering, please keep our core values in mind:
principles:
  - number: 1
    title: Lead with Kindness
    body: Be kind to everyone; you never know who might be going through a difficult
      time.
  - number: 2
    title: Celebrate Diversity
    body: Respect all people and all identities. We love and celebrate diversity
      here, everyone is a "Lady."
  - body: It’s just a game. While we love the adrenaline of countering a spell, our
      passion for our decks should never make our opponents feel humiliated.
    title: Keep it in Perspective
    number: 3
  - title: No Judgments
    number: 4
    body: Do not judge any Lady by her physical appearance. The reality is, we’re
      all divine.
  - body: Silence is a form of complicity. If you witness disrespect, please report
      it.
    title: Speak Up
    number: 5
  - number: 6
    body: We don't judge the condition of your cards or check their price tags. If
      you want to play with proxies, you are more than welcome.
    title: Proxies are Welcome
  - body: Just because you understand a complex card interaction doesn't mean you
      need to be condescending. Let's make learning the game fun for everyone.
    title: No "Womansplaining"
    number: 7
  - body: Avoid saying "my deck doesn't do that" when it actually does. We love a
      competitive deck and enjoy raising the bar, but games are only fair and
      fun when we are honest about our power levels and experience.
    title: Be Honest About Power Levels
    number: 8
  - body: Do not leak private or sensitive information to groups outside of Ladies
      The Gathering. We build this safe space on mutual trust.
    title: Privacy is Paramount
    number: 9
  - body: Our Ladies' Council is dedicated to giving you the best experience
      possible. Please share your feedback or complaints with our Council
      Members; they work alongside Lady Vanessa to ensure the best decisions are
      made for you and the entire community.
    title: We Are Here for You
    number: 10
footer_text: Sealed and decreed by the community. Written by Lady Vanessa
