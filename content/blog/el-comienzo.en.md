Once upon a time, there was a young sorceress named Vanessa, whose powers lay in her ability to cast spells through cards. For her, the magical world was a place full of knowledge where she could not only play with her enchantments, but also laugh and learn with her friends from the kingdom. Everything was in relative order; the hours spent exploring her magic with her friends passed quickly. However, as the years went by, laughter became echoes, friends; though loyal and skilled sorcerers faded into the sands of time, the atmosphere sometimes lost its luster, and certain creatures did not appreciate that the kingdom was a beautiful and precious place.

The desire to find more sorceresses with whom she could share the love she felt for her world became present, and so her adventure began. She searched across planes, mountains, islands, forests, and even swamps, seeking beings full of magic and light. During her journey, nervousness and doubt consumed her, but just when she thought she would never find anyone, she came across four warriors gathered together. It was at that very moment that she felt motivated to seek more brave women who wished to come together to learn about magic. The call came in the form of a "Commander Tea Party" meeting in which all the women from all the kingdoms could gather not only to play and learn about magic, but also to share a warm cup of tea and savor the delicacies of the realm. Fifteen sorceresses, queens, and warriors from all places arrived; the moment was indescribable, filled with emotion and above all, respect and safety. The meeting that began as an adventure became a naming ceremony, as all those who joined would be called Ladies The Gathering. Vanessa was no longer just a sorceress; now she was Lady Vanessa. Her love for magic grew, the echoes ceased to exist, spells became fun again, and at last she found the light she had yearned for.

Each month, the call spread from voice to voice, from parchment to parchment. The ladies loved discovering more young powerful beings full of magical gifts. Everything continued to grow and expand far beyond what Lady Vanessa had ever imagined, so much so that she began to require help from her friends, establishing the Council of Ladies, who are in charge of supporting the "Commander Tea Party" meetings.

This tale has not yet ended. Magic still lives on, the ladies stronger than ever, and affection continues to grow with great respect among each and every one of them. Conquering the world and continuing to gather more magical beings is the dream that is becoming a reality.\
\
The End.
